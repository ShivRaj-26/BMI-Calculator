BMI Introduction

A BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator is a tool that assesses an individual's body weight relative to their height. It's calculated by dividing a person's weight in kilograms by the square of their height in meters. The resulting number is categorized into different ranges, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity, helping to provide a general indication of one's health status in terms of weight. However, it doesn't directly measure body fat percentage or overall health, and should be considered alongside other health factors for a comprehensive evaluation.
